The Sarayu FAQs

Essential answers regarding RERA authenticity, possession times, and payment models.

What is HOABL The Sarayu Ayodhya?

The Sarayu is an elite, 7-star branded residential plotted development by The House of Abhinandan Lodha. It is situated on the sacred banks of the Sarayu River, offering 100% clear-title luxury villa plots in Ayodhya.

Is the project RERA registered?

Yes, the project is officially registered under Uttar Pradesh RERA. The official registration number is UPRERAPRJ397318/10/2024.

What is the starting price and size of plots?

Our premium plotted estates start at 1,250 Sq.Ft for ₹1.89 Cr* and 1,507 Sq.Ft at ₹2.16 Cr* (All-Inclusive package). For detailed layout files, payment schedules, and exact site plans, please submit an enquiry.

How far is the project from Shri Ram Mandir?

The Sarayu plotted enclave is situated within a convenient 15-minute drive from the main Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Temple complex and only 30 minutes from the Maharishi Valmiki International Airport.

Are site visits available?

Yes! We organize private chauffeur-driven site visits for qualified investors and their families. Please register via our enquiry forms to coordinate your schedule with our project expert.
